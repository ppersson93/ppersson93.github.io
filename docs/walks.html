Walks

At present London is in lockdown so I am not going on many walks but back when I did these are a few I went on and enjoyed.

Flitwick Circular Walk
Ingatestone to Wickford
Sawbridgeworth to Bishops Stortford
Lewes to Brighton

I avoid walking on a Sunday as there's always some horrid rail replacement bus scheduled and I recommend bringing a sandwich or at least an apple and a chocolate bar because sometimes the pubs listed in the guides have long been shuttered. Otherwise I cannot speak highly enough of the country walk as an institution.
